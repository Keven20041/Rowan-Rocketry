About Rowan Rocketry

Rowan Rocketry is a student-led organization dedicated to advancing aerospace technology and participating in high-powered rocketry competitions. Our team consists of passionate engineering students working on cutting-edge projects in propulsion, avionics, and more.

Our Achievements

  • Participated in the NASA Student Launch Initiative
  • Developed a custom hybrid rocket engine
  • Achieved altitudes of over 10,000 feet with our rockets
  • Won awards for innovation and team spirit in national competitions

Sponsorship Opportunities

By sponsoring Rowan Rocketry, you'll be investing in the future of aerospace engineering and supporting the next generation of innovators. We offer various sponsorship levels:

  • Tier 1: $500 - Your Organization's logo on our website
  • Tier 2: $500 - $1,000 - All lower tier perks + Logo on our apparel
  • Tier 3: $1500 - $2,500 - All lower tier perks + Logo on our rocket + social media recognition
  • Tier 4: $2,500 - All lower tier perks + Larger logo on our competition rocket + Larger logo on team apparel
