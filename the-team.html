Our Team

The Rowan University Rocketry Team is a dedicated group of mechanical and electrical engineering juniors and seniors, working both in and out of the classroom as part of our junior and senior clinic projects.


Divided into specialized sub-teams: airframe, avionics, payload, recovery and propulsion, we collaborate on every aspect of the rocket’s development. Whether it's designing structural components, developing electrical systems, or building a rover to deploy from the payload, we’re committed to pushing the boundaries of engineering while preparing for future careers in the aerospace industry.
